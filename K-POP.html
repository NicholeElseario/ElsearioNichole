Having thoughts of meeting your favourite celebrity?





You could say this place is a happy place for EXO fans, SHINee, F (x), Super Junior, Red Veltet, SNSD, NCT and other SM Entertainment artists. SMTOWN Land is not a playground with a variety of exciting rides, but this place seems to be made to pamper SM Entertainment artists Fan. Located in Coexartium, Samsung-dong, Seoul, South Korea. SMTOWN Land has six floors that bring a different sensation. Welcome Zone on the first floor, this place is one of the places decorated with large screens. The SM Entertainment artist’s videos are featured on these screens. In an alley, there’s an escalator that will take you to the second floor. On the second floor you can find “SUM”(Celebrity Shop) for you who want to buy various merchandise artists from SM Entertainment. If you want to get closer and know how the behind-the-scenes activities of SM artists go up to the third floor: SMTOWN Studio. Duplicates from the location of the video clip shoot are here, including the recording studio, the photo studio and the artist’s dance workroom surrounded by a mirrored wall. On the 4th floor, you can relax while listening to music and eating snacks. This place is named SUM Cafe. Do not stop there, but there is also SMTOWN Theater on the 5th and 6th floors. On this floor you can watch hologram musicals and concerts. Panavision large screen, photo box and 3D printing to print mini sculpture are also available on this floor. SMTOWN Land seems to be the perfect place for Kpop fans especially SM Entertainment artists fans. The address of the first building on the left is: 106-16 Cheongdam-dong, Gangnam-gu, Seoul, South Korea. And the address of the second building on the right is: 3 Huiujeong-ro 1-gil, Mapo-gu, Seoul.
